Toiminimi Toiset Aijat will have its own stand at the **Kuulutko sukuuni** ("Are you in my family?") genealogy fair in Vantaa on 10–11 October 2015.

This is a chance to buy books directly without postage costs, and to discuss genealogy and the research topics that Toiset Aijat specialises in. Come along if you have questions about Finnish cavalry, old court records, parish registers, or anything else connected to the publications.
