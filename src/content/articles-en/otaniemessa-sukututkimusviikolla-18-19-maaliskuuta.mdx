The Finnish Genealogical Society (*Suomen Sukututkimusseura*) is celebrating its 100th anniversary this year. The whole of week 11 — 13–19 March — is a nationwide genealogy celebration across Finland.

Toiminimi Toiset Aijat will be present on Saturday and Sunday **18–19 March in Otaniemi, Espoo** at the central event of the celebration week. Come along to browse books and discuss Finnish cavalry, court records, parish registers, or anything connected to genealogy and local history research.
