I will be at **Genealogy Day** in Tampere's Sampola community centre on **Saturday 28 January 2017**, with the newest book and earlier publications on display and for sale.

Travel is by bus, so the number of books I can bring is limited — but I will have a selection of titles available. Looking forward to meeting anyone who wants to discuss genealogy research, Finnish cavalry history, or the publications of Toiminimi Toiset Aijat.

More information about the event at [tamsuku.fi](https://www.tamsuku.fi).
