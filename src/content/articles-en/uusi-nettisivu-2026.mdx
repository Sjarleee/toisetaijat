The Toiset Aijat website has been redesigned. The new site is intended to be clearer and easier to use than the previous blog.

## Index

One of the key new features is the **index**. The index brings together personal and family information from Matti J. Kankaanpää's research materials. You can use it to find out whether people or families from your own ancestry appear in the research.

[Go to the index →](/en/hakemisto)

## MobilePay payment

Ordering books and articles is now easier. Orders can be paid with **MobilePay** — a fast and secure payment method. Payment is made directly with your phone, without bank credentials.

Orders are found on the for-sale page, which also provides detailed delivery information.

[See books and articles for sale →](/en/for-sale)

The new site is still being developed. If you notice anything missing or have wishes regarding the content, please get in touch.
