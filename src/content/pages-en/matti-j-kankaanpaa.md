Matti J. Kankaanpää (1943–2017) was a Finnish genealogy researcher and author from Virrat. He specialised in the history of Finnish cavalry regiments and regional genealogy in the Tampere area.

Over the course of several decades he compiled extensive genealogical indexes and published multiple books on Finnish military history, parish records and local history.
