### Personal Data Collected and Purpose

#### Orders

When placing an order, we collect the following information:

- Name
- Postal address (for book orders)
- Email address (for order confirmation and delivery)
- Phone number (if required for delivery)

This information is used solely to fulfil your order. It is not used for marketing and is not shared with third parties except to the extent required for processing payment and delivery.

Order data is stored in a secure Google Sheets spreadsheet used for order management. Google's privacy policy: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### Payment – MobilePay

Payments are processed via MobilePay (Vipps MobilePay AS). The information required for the payment transaction (name, phone number) is passed to Vipps MobilePay AS for payment processing. Vipps MobilePay AS acts as an independent data controller for this data under its own privacy policy.

More information: [MobilePay privacy](https://www.mobilepay.fi/tietosuoja)

### Cookies and Local Storage

#### Functional (no consent required)

**Browser LocalStorage – shopping cart:** The contents of your shopping cart are stored in your browser's local storage (localStorage). The data remains on your device only and is not sent to any server.

**Cloudflare:** The site operates through Cloudflare's CDN network. Cloudflare may set technical cookies (e.g. `cf_clearance`) to ensure security and performance. These are strictly necessary cookies and do not require consent. More information: [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

#### Analytics and advertising cookies (consent required)

These cookies are only loaded if you accept cookies via the site's cookie banner.

**Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to track visitor statistics. Google collects data about site usage via cookies. More information: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Google AdSense:** We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies for ad personalisation. More information: [Google Advertising Privacy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

#### Withdrawing consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by using your browser's private browsing mode.

### Data Retention

Personal data from orders is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the order and handle any complaints, but no longer than required by accounting legislation (6 years).

- restrict or object to the processing of your data
- request portability of your data to another controller
- lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority ([tietosuoja.fi](https://tietosuoja.fi))
