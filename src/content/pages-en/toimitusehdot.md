**Delivery**

Physical books are posted from Norway or during visits to Finland. Delivery times vary – current shipping dates are announced on the for-sale page.

**Payment**

Ebooks are available via Google Play Books. Physical books are ordered by email; payment instructions will be provided on confirmation.

**Returns**

If a book arrives damaged, please contact us within 14 days. We will arrange a replacement or refund.
